Tawa Pulao Recipe!

A popular and spicy street style pulao recipe made with long grain rice and pav bhaji masala. It is a widely appreciated quick pulao recipe in india, originated from the local mumbai streets. It is spicy and hence served with yoghurt raita, but also tastes great just by itself.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Pavbhaji Masala
  • Rice

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Stir fry the onion.
  2. Add tomatoes and capsicum and wait for 5-7 minutes till they are cooked.
  3. Add the Pavbhaji Masala.
  4. Add steamed rice and mix it properly.
  5. Garnish with lemon and coriander before serving.
