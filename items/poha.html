Poha Recipe - Maharashtrian Style!

Poha recipe is a popular Maharashtrian breakfast made from flattened rice, herbs and spices. It is a staple breakfast in our home which we all love.

Ingredients

  • Poha
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Stir fry the onions.
  2. Add tomatoes and let it cook for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add the spices in the appropriate amount
  4. Add poha and mix it well.
  5. Garnish with curry leaves before serving.
