Veg. Biryani!

This Veg Biryani is an authentic Indian vegetarian recipe packed full of your favorite rice, veggies, and spices. Made with patience and lots of love, this delicious veg dum biryani requires both care and attention to detail. The savory flavors in your first bite are well worth the time and effort spent in preparing it.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Capsicum
  • Tomatoes
  • Flower
  • Rice
  • Biryani Spices

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Stir fry the onions.
  2. Add the rest of the vegetables and let them cook for 10-12 minutes.
  3. Add the spices in the appropriate proportion.
  4. Add steamed rice and mix it with the gravy properly.
  5. Garnish it with onions and cashewnuts before serving.
